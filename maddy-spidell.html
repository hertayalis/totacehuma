There are many personalities who are famous for using social media accounts positively. Among others, Maddy Spidell is also the one who is gathering wide media attention as an Instagram star. Spidell shares fashion-friendly, traveling, dance photos & videos on her Instagram account.

The gorgeous Maddy was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, the United States of America, on March 31, 2000. Maddy is the daughter of Mary Spidell (mother) and Michael Spidell (father). She has two brothers named Jedd Spidell and Thad Spidell. 

Regarding education, Maddy attended Greendale High School. She studies at the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee. 

Who Is Maddy Spidell's Boyfriend?

The 20-years-old social media influencer Maddy is in a romantic relationship with Jimmy Donaldson famous as Mr. Beast. Maddy's boyfriend Jimmy is the successful You-Tuber who has amassed 43 million subscribers in his entertainment You-Tube channel (MrBeast).

The Instagram star Spidell and Mr. Beast are dating since June 2019. The duo is sharing a good bond as a perfect love partner. Back on February 14, 2020, on the occasion of Valentine's Day, Jimmy surprised his girlfriend Maddy with 100 thousand roses. 

The lovebirds (Jimmy & Maddy) spends their quality time together. They visit many exotic places together and the pair also never hesitate to flaunt romantic pictures on their respective social accounts.

Philanthropic Works Via You-Tube

The You-Tub star Mr. Beast is not only collecting an impressive sum of wealth, but the guy with a pure heart is also involved in several philanthropic works. Back on October 25, 2019, on You-Tube Mr. Beast and the fellow You-Tuber Mark Rober created a fundraising event called Team Trees.

The main mission of the scheme was to collect $20 million for the Arbor Day Foundation, by planting 20 million trees. Fortunately, the project received a large donation which reached over $22 million. Some of the donors were Tobias Lutke, Elon Musk, Susan Wojcicki (CEO of You-Tube), and others.
