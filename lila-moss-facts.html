    We're still reeling from the drama of this year's Met Gala. From Choupette tributes to lashings of pearls, fashion's biggest night of the calendar certainly lived up to expectation. But our outfit highlight? Lila Moss and her mum Kate who rocked up to the legendary event in matching Fendi, vying for the label of fashion's chicest mother-daughter duo.

    WATCH: Kate Moss’s Most Iconic Fashion Looks

    A definite rising star in the industry, Lila graced the steps of the Metropolitan Museum of Art in an uber-fluffy midi with a sheer midriff panel, simultaneously proving just how much smouldering looks do run in the Moss family.

    Following in her mother's footsteps, Lila is set to be the next big thing in the fashion world. The rising star made her runway debut walking in Miu Miu's SS21 show and since then has wasted no time in topping up her portfolio. She has walked in Jacquemus' Le Papier show as well as the internet breaking Versace and Fendi crossover show, and has starred in campaigns for the likes of Calvin Klein, Marc Jacobs, Chloé and even high street hero Zara.

    Taking to the catwalk in her own way and by her own rules, Lila is certainly one to watch, following her own path under the guidance of mother Kate. But what else is there to know about her? Lucky for you, Hello! Fashion has done the research so you don’t have to. Keep scrolling to find out more about Lila Moss...

    What is Lila Moss' full name and how old is she? 

    Her full name is Lila Moss Grace Hack, and she was born on 29th September 2002, which makes her 20 years old.

    MORE: Lila Moss and Mia Regan turn up the glamour at the Royal Academy Of Arts Summer Exhibition

    Who is part of Lila Moss' family? 

    We're not sure if we mentioned it, but Lila is the daughter of legendary supermodel Kate Moss. Hence, the apple doesn't fall far from the tree. Lila's father is curator and creative director Jefferson Hack, and he is also the co-founder of print and digital platform Dazed Media. Up until recently, Lila resided in Highgate Village in north London with her mother, but she now lives with her father in Islington. Lila is an only child. 

    Who are Lila Moss' closest friends?

    Lila’s closest friends stem from her childhood - her best friends being Iris Law, daughter of Jude Law and Sadie Frost, and Stella Jones, daughter of Mick Jones from The Clash and Miranda Davis.

    Does Lila Moss have diabetes?

    Yes, and Lila is also an advocate for other people living with Type 1 diabetes, having been spotted sporting her insulin pump with unadulterated confidence at several fashion events and shows.

    View post on Instagram 

    She also has appeared in several campaigns, including a joint fashion campaign for Versace and Fendi which saw her posing in a cream patterned sleeveless shift dress, black and gold strappy sandals and a miniature handbag. Also visible in the photo on Lila's upper arm is her glucose monitoring device which she uses to check her blood sugar levels.

    "I think not many people know that I have diabetes. It's not visible from the outside, so no one would really know just by looking at you," Lila told in 2020.
