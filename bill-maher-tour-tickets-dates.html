BILL Maher, star of HBO's Real Time - an award-winning, news-talk series - is on his 21st season of the show.

The comedian is, once again, embarking on a laugh-filled adventure during his 2023 tour.

Is Bill Maher on tour?

Bill Maher's comedy tour began on April 1, 2023, and will be going through October 8.

His first stop was in Paramount Theater, in Seattle, Washington, while his last stop is expected to be at the Orpheum Theater, in Omaha, Nevada.

The 67-year-old will be visiting states across the country including Texas, Pennsylvania, North Carolina, and South Carolina.

Maher will also be performing live at the David Copperfield Theater at MGM Grand Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas for a total of four days - September 15 and 16, as well as, November 3 and 4.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets to see the Emmy award winner are currently available via Ticketmaster.

A list of all his shows can be found on his official website.

Ticket prices may vary according to venue and space available.
