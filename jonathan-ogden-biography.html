Who is Jonathan Ogden?

Jonathan Ogden is a former American Football offensive tackle. Throughout his career in NFL, he played for the Baltimore Ravens. Previously, he played college football for the University of California, Los Angeles.

Jonathan Ogden: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Ogden was born in Washington, D.C on July 31, 1974, to parents Cassandra Sneed and Shirrel Phillip Ogden. As of 2022, he is 48 years old. He became interested in the world of football in his childhood years. He is of American nationality. Additionally, there are no details regarding his ethnic background at present.

Education

Talking about his education, Ogden attended St. Albans School in Washington. Later, graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) and majored in history.

Jonathan Ogden: Professional life, Career

Ogden initially became an important player for UCLA and helped his team to the 1993 Pac-10 Championship and Rose Bowl.

Additionally, he won the 1996 NCAA Men’s Division I Indoor Track and Field Championships. Coming to his professional career, the Baltimore Ravens drafted Ogden in the 1996 NFL Draft. He played in three of the NFL seasons.

Ogden won a Super Bowl ring with the Ravens in 2001. Eventually, he announced his retirement on June 12, 2008. In addition, he was the Ravens’ honorary captain at Super Bowl XLVII. After his footballing career, Ogden has also been part of the WWE universe. Furthermore, he has also appeared in commercials for Apple Ford.
